PHASE 2B: 2 AND 3 FLOOR CONSTRUCTION

Completed in 2013-2014

Phase 2B began in 2013 with Asbestos abatement on the 2nd and 3rd Floors.Â  Other major projects completed during this phase included the demolition and replacement of the mechanical equipment in the east and west penthouse fan rooms.

When this phase was completed in 2014, the 2nd and 3rd Floors were equipped with signature reading rooms, graduate study rooms, new book stacks, comfortable reader spaces and bathrooms.

Additionally, access was provided to the spectacular tower area, which now serves as a reading room.

Patrons are now enjoying these beautifully renovated areas while construction continues on the lower floors.

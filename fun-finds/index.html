Vintage Travel Brochure Found…

This 1957 Havana travel brochure was found in a study carrel during recent demolition work.

Original Faculty Lounge Draperies Discovered…

These drapery panels were found inside of a locked closet in the Seipp Alcove on the 3rd Floor. The key was nowhere to be found and when the workmen demolished a wall adjacent to the alcove, they found the drapes. We believe they are the same drapes used in the original faculty lounge in 1948.
